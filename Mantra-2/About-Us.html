About Us

Mantra Bar & Kitchen is the newest addition to the Doha’s Social Experience, featuring inside seating as well as outside balcony overlooking West Bay Skyline and an Arabian sea. Perfect Place to hang out, with great food and beverages including Choice of Beer, Spirits and our creative cocktails & mocktails. Inside features a great atmosphere with lounge and bar set up, perfect lighting and a great music to lift your mood.Mantra Bar & Kitchen is a sophisticated space where you can enjoy delicious concoctions. We serve variety of dishes specially designed by our chefs, which includes continental soup, salad, burger, pasta and tandoori dishes with the twist.  Enjoy Our Daily Happy Hours from 3pm to 8pm with reduced rates on special beverages.
